## Bonus - Interview Skills 

### Communication 
1. Speaking in frameworks, most popular STAR method.
2. Solving problems - They are more focus on what problem did you solve more than what project you built.
3. Short and subtle - Just answer in simple and short unless they ask. NO jorgons, no complicated words and so on. Keep it simple plain english.

Use AI to practise, AI isn't bad it helps you to achieve your goals must faster. Just embrace it. give sometime to learn how to use AI and you will be unstoppable.
